Max Temperature


As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approches the equator (0 De. Latitude). More interestingly. however, is the fact that the souther hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the norther hemisphere. This may be due the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
